This is a collection of some design projects I worked on. These represent the way I think in order to solve a problem, my enthusiasm for continuously learning and simply put, they illustrate the production of a concept.

I am a self-taught graphic designer. I have been improving my skill set by taking various courses online such as from Treehouse, Tuts Plus and earlier this year I attended a short programme of study at WAES college in London. I am proficient in using Adobe Creative Cloud software: Photoshop, Indesign and especially Illustrator.

Also I acquired a reasonable knowledge of HTML and CSS by coding this website from scratch.

Curious to know why I call myself an undercover designer? Well, there are many answers to this question but the truth is that I have not attended my debutante ball quite yet!

My story started some time ago when I realised how much I love to create. Though what excites me the most is the process behind the scene which is the visualisation of an idea translating into something concrete.

So I decided to start learning how to do that through graphics. I have always been passionate about all forms of expression such as arts. Human behaviour also really intrigues me and by adding functionality to the mix I chose to pursue a career as a designer.

When I am not designing I like to enjoy a good book or wander around snapping shots of new places or at the moment, having a crack at learning Russian.
