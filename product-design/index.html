01

vector portraits

product design

Commission. While exploring the world of character design I decided to learn to create portraits with Illustrator. After posting the first one on Instagram I started to receive requests to make some for others. By using solid colours, highlights and shadows I managed to obtain a satisfactory 3D effect.

02

book cover designs

product design

Assignment. These are the book cover designs I created in order to represent four collections at the Royal Victoria and Albert museum. For the whole of the process I worked hard to maintain consistency across all the book covers. The concept I aimed to express is based on the evolution of design throughout centuries.

03

oyster card wallet

product design

Personal project. I produced this wallet design purposely playing around the concept of an oyster being associated to a way of travelling in London. By applying a cartoon-like style and colours I underlined the amusement of the overall project.
