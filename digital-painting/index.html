01

on the mirror

digital painting

Personal illustration. I find Surrealism to be very intriguing both as a concept and as the final visualisation on canvas. It allows the viewer to possess more freedom of interpretation and also it builds a set of boundaries around it. This canvas is the projection of one of my ideas about the relationship between a woman and her mirror.

02

drinking perspective

digital painting

Personal illustration. Exploring perspective as a form of art. By using different perspectives we can convey various messages. Knowing the message we want to deliver before starting our composition, makes our final work even more effective. Instead of concentrating on the characters of the composition I prioritize the act.
