playing with fonts

poster design

Personal illustration. Words are one of the many ways to communicate and fonts are an essential functional aesthetic to reach out to people. I used a variety of bold solid colours to create a vibrant and interesting background and to allow the cut outs to pop. This poster design was also selected for the 'WAES College Summer Exhibition 2018'.

02

london fashion week

poster design

Assignment. To make a poster for the London fashion week, I decided to go for something different. There is a lot of controversy in the fashion world. Models are seen as mannequins with the only purpose to advertise the latest clothing line. Keeping this in mind I created an illustration that would represent both persons and clothes on the same level.
